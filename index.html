Agra - The City of Taj

About Agra

Agra is famous for being the home of one of the Seven Wonders of the World, the Taj Mahal. This magnificent marble mausoleum was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in the 17th century as a tribute to his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal. Apart from the Taj Mahal, Agra is also known for other historical and architectural marvels. One such example is the Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site that served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors until the capital was shifted to Delhi. The fort complex includes several impressive structures, such as palaces, halls, and mosques.
